Homemade Chicken Wontons

with Spicy Chili Sauce

Time: 30 minutes

4 servings (30/40 wontons)

Ingredients

For the Wontons

  1. Ground chicken (450g / 1lb)
  2. Soy sauce (1 tbsp)
  3. Sesame oil (1 tsp)
  4. Shaoxing rice wine (1 tbsp)
  5. Fresh ginger (1 tsp)
  6. Minced garlic (2 cloves)
  7. Green onion (2, chopped)
  8. Cornstarch (1 tsp)
  9. A pinch of pepper (white or black)
  10. Wonton wrappers, squares preferred (1 pack)
  11. Water (small bowl)

For the Sauce

  1. Chili oil (2-3 tbsp)
  2. Soy sauce (2 tbsp)
  3. Vinegar, Chinikiang preferred (1 tbsp)
  4. Minced garlic (2 cloves)
  5. Sugar (1/2 tsp)
  6. Sesame oil (1 tsp)
  7. Toasted sesame seeds (1 tsp)
  8. Green onion (1, sliced)

Recipe

1. Make the Sauce

In your main bowl, whisk together the minced garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil and sugar until the sugar dissolves

Stir in the chili oil (with plenty of flakes), sesame seeds and the sliced green onions.

2. Mix the Filling

In a medium bowl, mis the ground chicken, soy sauce, sesame oil, wice wine, ginger, garlic, green onions, cornstarch and pepper

Mix in one direction until the mixture becomes slightl sticky

2. Wrap the Wontons

With a wonton wrapper on your hand or on a cutting board, put about 1 teaspoon of filling in the center

Dip your finger in water and wet all fout edges of the wrapper

Fold the wrapper in half diagonally to form a triangle, press the edges firmly and bring the two bottom corners together to form a "nurse cap" fold

3. Cook Them Up and Assemble

Cook them in batches of 10 - 15 so they don't crowd

Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil. Drop the wontons GENTLY

Stir gently once so they don't stick to the bottom.

Once they're floating at the top, let them cook for 2-3 more minutes and remove the wontons with a slotted spoon, daining all the water

Drop them directly into the sauce bowl

How to Serve

Gently toss the wontons in the bowl to coat them completely in the sauce

Spoon extra sauce over the top and garnish with a few extra scallions
